Scrabble

  • New: to start a new game
  • Ready: to deal tiles
  • Submit: to calculate score and end turn
  • Click tiles to activate tile (Green = Activated)
  • If Activated: click on board to place tile
  • If tile is on the board. Click on it to remove.
  • NO VALIDATIONS. BRING DICTIONARY. PLAY AT YOUR OWN RISK.
